Introduction to the 43 Problems

Brauer's problem 20(The $k(B)$ Conjecture): Is it true that a $p$-block of defect $d$ consists of $p^d$ irreducible characters?
More formally, let $B$ be a $p$-block of a finite group $G$ with defect group $D$. Does the number of irreducible ordinary characters in $B$, denoted $k(B)$, satisfy $k(B) \le |D|$.
